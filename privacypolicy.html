Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how shadownik, a cybersecurity services provider, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to transparency and fairness in our handling of your personal data.

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as:

  • Your name and contact information (email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Information about your organization (company name, job title, etc.)
  • Information about your interactions with our website and services (IP address, browser type, etc.)
  • Any other information you provide to us through our website or services
  • Device information, such as device type, operating system, and browser type
  • Location information, such as your country, region, or city
  • Cookies and other tracking technologies, such as Google Analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide our cybersecurity services to you, including threat detection, incident response, and security consulting
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you newsletters and promotional materials (if you have opted-in)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Conduct market research and analysis
  • Develop and improve our products and services

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or use. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and data storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Training and awareness programs for our employees
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or update your personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to our processing of your personal information
  • Request a copy of your personal information in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
